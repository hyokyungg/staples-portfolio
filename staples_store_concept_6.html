Design Decisions
Navy station body, orange counter edge
The dark station against a warm cream wall creates a focal point that draws the eye from the entrance. Orange edge stripe echoes the brand promise — a frame of warmth around technical precision.
Monitor pre-loaded from app on arrival
Maya sits down and her file is already there. The digital-to-physical sync happens invisibly — this is the waveline moment where frustration becomes confidence.
Print sample slot on station edge
The physical test strip slots into a holder at eye height. Maya can hold it next to her screen at the exact same moment. Colour truth, not colour faith.
Oak stool, pendant light, warm materials
Even a technical station feels like a studio, not a repair shop. The material palette — oak, warm cream, navy — signals craft, not convenience.
"This is the room no other stationery store has. This is the moat."
